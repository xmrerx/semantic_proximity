( reuters ) - irma , now a category 4 , is heading towards the leeward islands in the caribbean and is expected to move near or over the northern part of the region by tuesday night or wednesday , the u.s. national hurricane center said .
hurricane irma is about 320 miles ( 515 km ) east of the leeward islands and packing maximum sustained winds of 150 mph ( 240 km/h ) , the miami-based weather forecaster said .
swells generated by irma will affect the northern leeward islands , puerto rico , and the u.s. and british virgin islands during the next several days , the nhc said .
