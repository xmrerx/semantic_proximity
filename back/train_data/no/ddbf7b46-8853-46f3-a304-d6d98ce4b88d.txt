paris ( reuters ) - france warned iraq s kurdish region on wednesday that a declaration of independence would likely destabilise the region , but called on the central iraqi government to give the kurds greater autonomy .
what would be desirable today is that kurdish iraq be integrated in the reconstruction of iraq , reconciliation of iraq by having the greatest amount of autonomy possible , foreign minister jean-yves le drian told bfm tv .
it does not seem timely today to have independence ... but ( if ) a declaration of independence happened it would provoke new major crises in the middle east at a time when islamic state is being defeated in iraq .
