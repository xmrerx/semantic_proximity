washington ( reuters ) - president donald trump was speaking on tuesday with a number of middle east leaders , the white house said , in advance of his expected announcement about the u.s. embassy in israel .
trump had spoken to or planned to speak to israeli prime minister benjamin netanyahu , palestinian president mahmoud abbas and jordan s king abdullah , white house spokeswoman sarah sanders said .
the president has calls scheduled this morning with prime minister netanyahu , king abdullah of jordan and palestinian authority president abbas .
we will have a readout on these calls later today , sanders said .
trump was expected to announce as soon as wednesday that he will again delay moving the u.s. embassy in tel aviv to jerusalem but stress that he will wants to do so , a senior administration official said .
the official said trump was also likely to say that the united states recognizes jerusalem as the capital of israel .
