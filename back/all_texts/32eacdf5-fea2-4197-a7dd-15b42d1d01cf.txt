It was just announced today that the Obama s are in the middle of a bidding war by publishers for their memoirs.The rights for Barack and Michelle Obama s memoirs will sell for at least $60 million, according to a report from the Financial Times.The eventual total price tag will include a book from both the former President and First Lady.
The couple plans to write their memoirs separately, but sell the rights of their finished accounts together as a package, according to the Financial Times report.
Penguin Random House, who published former President Obama s three other books, reportedly leads the chase, but HarperCollins, Simon & Schuster and Macmillan are also in the hunt, according to the Financial Times.
