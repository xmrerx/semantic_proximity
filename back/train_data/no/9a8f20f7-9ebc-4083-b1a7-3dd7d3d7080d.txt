astana ( reuters ) - syrian groups who choose to boycott the russia-sponsored national congress next month risk being sidelined as the political process moves ahead , russian negotiator alexander lavrentyev said on tuesday .
lavrentyev said the most important task of the congress was launching a constitutional reform .
lavrentyev spoke to reporters after talks with turkish and iranian delegations , as well as syrian government and rebel groups , in kazakhstan which he said failed to produce finalised prisoner swap documents .
