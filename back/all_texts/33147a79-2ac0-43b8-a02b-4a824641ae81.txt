MOSCOW (Reuters) - Media outlets such as CNN, Radio Free Europe and Deutsche Welle could be affected by Russia s new foreign agents media law, Andrei Isayev, a senior lawmaker from the ruling United Russia party, told RIA news agency on Monday.
Russia s parliament warned last week that some U.S. and other foreign media outlets could be declared foreign agents and obliged to regularly declare full details of their funding, finances and staffing.
