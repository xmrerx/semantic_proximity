luxembourg ( reuters ) - german foreign minister sigmar gabriel warned on monday that threats from u.s. president donald trump to pull out from the iran nuclear accord could provoke military confrontation , adding such a move could also make exacerbate the north korea crisis .
as europeans together , we are very worried that the decision of the u.s. president could lead us back into military confrontation with iran , gabriel told reporters ahead of a meeting with fellow european foreign ministers .
gabriel said he would be discussing how to save the deal - to which britain , france , germany , russia , china , iran and the european union are also signatories - with his european union colleagues .
senior trump administration officials said on sunday that the united states was committed to remaining part of the iran nuclear accord for now , despite trump s criticisms of the deal and his warnings that he might pull out .
