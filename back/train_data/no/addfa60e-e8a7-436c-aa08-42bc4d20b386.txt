tokyo ( reuters ) - north korea fired a ballistic missile over japan on friday morning , japan s government said , strongly protesting against what it called pyongyang s latest intolerable provocation .
the missile was launched at 6:57 a.m. japan time ( 2157 gmt ) , flew over hokkaido and splashed down at 7:16 a.m. ( 2216 gmt ) some 2,000 kilometres east of the northern island s cape erimo , said chief cabinet secretary yoshihide suga .
japan protests the latest launch in the strongest terms and will take appropriate and timely action at the united nations and elsewhere , staying in close contact with the united states and south korea , suga told reporters .
