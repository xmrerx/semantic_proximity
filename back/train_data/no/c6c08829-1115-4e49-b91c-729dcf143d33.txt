berlin ( reuters ) - the german government on friday vowed to work for continued global unity if u.s. president donald trump announces that he will not certify iran s compliance with a 2015 nuclear accord negotiated by world powers , as expected .
we have a great interest in the continuation of this international unity .
if ... an important country like the united states comes to a different conclusion as appears to be the case , we will work even harder with other partners to maintain this cohesion , government spokesman steffen seibert told reporters .
