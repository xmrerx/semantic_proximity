OSLO (Reuters) - Norway s Foreign Minister Boerge Brende has resigned from his position to become president of the World Economic Forum, he told a news conference on Friday Brende will keep his position until mid-October.
Before becoming foreign minister in 2013, he was the WEF s managing director in 2008-2009 and 2011-2013 Prime Minister Erna Solberg won re-election on Monday and is in talks to other parties to form a new government.
