washington ( reuters ) - the united states on thursday slapped sanctions on seven iranian individuals and two entities , alleging involvement in either malicious cyber activities or enabling tehran s ballistic missile program .
the action , announced on the u.s. treasury department s website , freezes any assets they may hold in the united states and prohibits u.s. individuals from doing business with them .
