washington ( reuters ) - canadian prime minister justin trudeau on thursday vowed to work with whomever replaces president barack obama when his term ends in january following the u.s. presidential election in november .
“ the relationship , the friendship between our two countries goes far beyond any two individuals or any ideology .
i have tremendous confidence in the american people and look forward to working with whomever they chose to send to this white house later this year , ” said trudeau , speaking at a white house press conference alongside obama , a democrat .
