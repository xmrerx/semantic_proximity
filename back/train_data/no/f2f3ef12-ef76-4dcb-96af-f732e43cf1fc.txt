berlin ( reuters ) - chancellor angela merkel has canceled a news conference with dutch prime minister mark rutte that had been planned for monday , her office said , after german coalition talks collapsed overnight .
the chancellery gave no reason for the cancellation of the news conference , which had been scheduled for 1 p.m. ( 1200 gmt ) .
merkel said earlier on monday her efforts to form a three-way coalition government had failed , thrusting germany into a political crisis .
