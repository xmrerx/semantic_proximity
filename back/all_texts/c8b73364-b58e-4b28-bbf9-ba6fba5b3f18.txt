MADRID (Reuters) - Spain s state prosecutor on Thursday asked the Spanish Supreme Court to jail the Catalan parliament s speaker and three lawmakers pending an investigation into their role in Catalonia s banned push for independence, El Pais reported.
The Catalan parliament speaker, Carmen Forcadell, and five regional lawmakers testified on Thursday on charges of sedition, rebellion and misuse of public funds The prosecutor asked the judge to release two other regional lawmakers, according to the newspaper.
It was not clear whether their release involved certain conditions.
