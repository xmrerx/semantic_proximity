madrid ( reuters ) - secessionist parties in the catalan parliament are discussing an independence declaration that will be submitted to the regional assembly next tuesday , a pro-independence catalan legislator was quoted as saying by el mundo newspaper on friday .
we are in talks about a text , with paper and pencil , on the declaration that we want the regional parliament to accept on tuesday , carles riera , a lawmaker from the cup ( popular unity candidacy ) , was quoted as saying .
nobody has put forward any scenario of delay , ambiguity or confusion .
we are not working on that scenario , he said .
