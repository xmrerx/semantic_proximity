berlin ( reuters ) - germany and france will ask the european union to consult about the possibility of imposing heavier sanctions on north korea after its latest nuclear test , germany s government spokesman steffen seibert said on monday .
it is exclusively north korea , its leadership , and president kim who are responsible for this provocation , seibert told a news conference , adding pyongyang s ambassador would again be summoned to the foreign ministry .
north korea is treading all over international law .
berlin was in favor of a diplomatic , peaceful solution to the crisis , he said , adding this required a coordinated response from the entire world - not just the western world , but russia and china too .
