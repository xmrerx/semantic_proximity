washington ( reuters ) - u.s. president donald trump on thursday praised the rescue of a kidnapped u.s.-canadian couple and their three children by the pakistani military , calling the effort a positive moment for u.s.-pakistan relations .
on wednesday , the u.s. and pakistani governments secured the release of the boyle-coleman family from captivity in pakistan .
today they are free , trump said in a statement released by the white house .
the pakistani government s cooperation is a sign that it is honoring america s wishes for it to do more to provide security in the region .
