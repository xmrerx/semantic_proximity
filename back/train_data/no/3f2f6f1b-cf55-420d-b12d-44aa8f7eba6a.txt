johannesburg ( reuters ) - the southern african development community ( sadc ) , an intergovernmental organization , said on friday that it was ready to work closely with zimbabwe s incoming leader emmerson mnangagwa and his government .
mnangagwa is due to be sworn in as zimbabwean president on friday following the resignation of robert mugabe , who had ruled zimbabwe since independence in 1980 .
sadc is a 16-country intergovernmental organization which is currently chaired by south africa .
