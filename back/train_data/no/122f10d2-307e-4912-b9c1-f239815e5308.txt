dubai ( reuters ) - saudi foreign minister adel al-jubeir said on wednesday his country will lend support to the syrian opposition to come out unified from talks in the saudi capital riyadh ahead of peace talks in geneva .
we will provide help and support for them in all what they need , jubeir told reporters in riyadh after he attended the opening session of a syrian opposition conference aimed at unify their ranks ahead of peace talks in geneva .
we hope they can come out of the conference unified , he added .
