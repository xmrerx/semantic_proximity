bucharest ( reuters ) - romania s former king michael died in switzerland at the age of 96 on tuesday , the royal house was quoted by digi 24 tv as saying on tuesday .
king michael of romania withdrew from public life because of illness in 2016 .
a cousin of britain s queen elizabeth , michael was forced to abdicate in 1947 after the post-war communist takeover of romania and has lived in exile in the west for decades .
he underwent surgery for leukaemia and cancer .
