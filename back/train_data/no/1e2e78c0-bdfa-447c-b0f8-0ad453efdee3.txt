benghazi , libya ( reuters ) - three workers , two of them sudanese , died in an accident at libya s zelten oil and gas field on saturday , an oil source said .
they died after inhaling gas during maintenance works , the source said , adding that a fourth person was being treated in hospital .
the nationality of the third dead was not immediately known .
the field belongs to sirte oil company and pumps between 35,000 and 40,000 barrels a day .
