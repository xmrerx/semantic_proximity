jakarta ( reuters ) - the indonesian unit of freeport-mcmoran inc has reopened the main supply route to its papuan mine , a company spokesman said on monday , after the road was closed on sunday following a shooting incident in the area .
it was already open this morning , freeport indonesia spokesman riza pratama told reuters , when asked about the road .
he said the closure had had no impact on production at the world s second biggest copper mine .
