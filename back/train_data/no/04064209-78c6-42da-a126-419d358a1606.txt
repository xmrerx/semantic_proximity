washington ( reuters ) - u.s. senate democratic leader chuck schumer said on tuesday that president donald trump s characterization of north korean leader kim jong un as rocket man during a speech to the united nations was risky .
if i were giving the president advice , i would have said avoid using rocket man , schumer said during a press conference at the u.s. capitol .
we know the leader of north korea is erratic , to put it kindly .
that kind of language i think is risky .
