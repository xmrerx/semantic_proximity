danang , vietnam ( reuters ) - the remaining 11 countries in the trans pacific partnership ( tpp ) trade deal will discuss a proposal for an agreement in principle to proceed after the withdrawal of the united states , japanese economy minister toshimitsu motegi said on thursday .
ministers from the 11 countries are meeting in the resort of danang in vietnam on the sidelines of the asia pacific economic cooperation ( apec ) summit .
we have collectively reached the stage where we can discuss a proposal for a final package for an agreement in principle of the tpp , motegi told ministers from the other countries .
i would like to emphasize once again the importance of reaching an agreement in principle right here .
