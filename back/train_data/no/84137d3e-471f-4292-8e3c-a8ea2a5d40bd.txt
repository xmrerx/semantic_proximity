dublin ( reuters ) - ireland is not ready to allow talks on britain s withdrawal from the european union to move to the second phase next month , foreign minister simon coveney said on tuesday .
yes we all want to move onto phase two of the brexit negotiations but we are not in a place right now that allows us to do that .
we have very serious issues , particularly around the ( uk-irish ) border , that need more clarity , coveney told a news conference before a meeting with his british counterpart boris johnson .
