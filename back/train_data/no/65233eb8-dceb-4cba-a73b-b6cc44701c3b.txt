washington ( reuters ) - republican party presidential candidate donald trump tweeted on tuesday that he raised $ 13 million in 24 hours from online donations .
trump , who has struggled to convince traditional republican donors to back his campaign , has turned to online donations from supporters to fund his campaign for the nov. 8 election .
in august , trump raised $ 90 million , trailing democratic party candidate hillary clinton , who raised $ 143 million in the same month .
