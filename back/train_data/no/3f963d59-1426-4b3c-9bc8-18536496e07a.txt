ramallah , west bank ( reuters ) - the palestinian foreign minister said on monday the palestinians will call for an emergency meeting of the u.n. general assembly after the u.s. vetoed a security council resolution calling for the withdrawal of its declaration that jerusalem is israel s capital .
president donald trump reversed decades of u.s. policy on dec. 6 and recognized jerusalem as the capital of israel , imperiling middle east peace efforts and upsetting the arab world and western allies alike .
we are moving within 48 hours ... to call for an emergency meeting of the general assembly , palestinian foreign minister riyad al-maliki told reporters in ramallah .
he said the international community would consider the decision by president trump as null and void .
