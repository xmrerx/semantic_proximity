ankara ( reuters ) - turkish police detained 25 suspected islamic state militants in operations across istanbul , the state-run anadolu news agency said on tuesday .
three of the suspects were high-level members of the militant group , anadolu said .
it said 22 of the suspected jihadists were foreign nationals .
ankara has detained more than 5,000 islamic state suspects and deported some 3,290 foreign militants from 95 different countries in recent years , according to turkish officials .
it has also refused entry to at least 38,269 individuals .
last week , turkish police shot dead an islamic state militant who was set to carry out a suicide bomb attack on a police station in the mediterranean city of mersin .
