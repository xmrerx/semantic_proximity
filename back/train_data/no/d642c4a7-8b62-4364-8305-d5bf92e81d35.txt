wellington ( reuters ) - votes cast for new zealand s ruling national party reached 46.3 percent with 31.4 percent of results counted in the country s general election on saturday , while support for the opposition labour party was 36 percent , according to the electoral commission .
the nationalist new zealand first party had 7.1 percent of the vote so far , tipping it as a likely kingmaker in the german-style proportional representation system .
votes for the green party reached 6.2 percent .
a record 1.2 million ballots were cast before the day of the election , accounting for about a third of the 3.3 million new zealanders enrolled to vote .
in past elections , advanced votes were indicative of final results .
