hanoi ( reuters ) - vietnamese police have arrested a dissident accused of attempting to overthrow the government , the communist state s official website said on wednesday , bringing to at least 17 the number of dissidents arrested this year .
tran thi xuan , 41 , was arrested in the northern province of ha tinh on tuesday .
the website did not give details of the alleged crime .
reuters was unable to contact her and it was not clear whether she had a lawyer .
despite sweeping economic reforms and growing openness to social change , the communist party tolerates little criticism .
the crackdown on dissent follows changes in the ruling party hierarchy early last year which gave greater influence to the security establishment .
