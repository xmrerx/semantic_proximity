budapest ( reuters ) - hungarian police have detained a pakistani migrant wanted for murdering around 70 people in his home country , hungarian and austrian police said on wednesday .
in a statement , police in hungary s bacs-kiskun county said they had stopped a group of illegal migrants near the southern border with serbia on tuesday and identified the 35-year-old man among them .
the police identified him only by the initials a.z .
and said he was the target of an international arrest warrant issued by pakistan .
they said he was being held in hungary but gave no further details .
austrian police issued a statement saying the arrest had been made after a tip-off from austria to hungarian authorities .
they said the man had been planning to go to austria with the help of people-smugglers .
they declined to identify him on grounds of austria s data protection rules .
