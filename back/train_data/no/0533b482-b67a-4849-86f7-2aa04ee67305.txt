amsterdam ( reuters ) - a dutchman wielding a knife was shot by military police on friday at amsterdam s schiphol airport and taken into custody , an official said .
there were no reports of other injuries .
the suspect in the incident is a 29-year-old man from the hague , police said in a tweet .
the man is known to the police in connection with previous violent incidents .
the man had made threats with a knife and was shot at the central plaza of schiphol airport , an area with shops and restaurants , spokesman stan verberkt said .
he was injured and has been taken into custody .
no details of his condition were released .
the immediate area around the incident was closed to the public , but authorities gave the all clear and the airport was operating as usual , he said .
