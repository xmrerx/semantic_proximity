moscow ( reuters ) - the kremlin on monday commented on u.s. president barack obama ’ s historic visit to cuba , saying it was in russia ’ s interests for havana , its long-time ally , to have good relations with the united states .
“ decades of friendly partner-like relations link russia and cuba , ” kremlin spokesman dmitry peskov told a teleconference with reporters .
“ we are interested in cuba , which is friendly to us , maintaining good relations with all its neighbors and above all with the united states. ” obama arrived to small but cheering crowds on sunday at the start of a historic visit to cuba that opened a new chapter in u.s. engagement with the island ’ s communist government after decades of hostility between the former cold war foes .
