london ( reuters ) - two men arrested on suspicion of belonging to a banned far-right group and planning terrorist acts have been released without charge , british police said on sunday .
the men were among five , including some serving soldiers , arrested on sept. 5 as part of a pre-planned , intelligence-led operation .
they were detained on suspicion of being involved in the commission , preparation and instigation of acts of terrorism and of being members of neo-nazi organization the national action group .
