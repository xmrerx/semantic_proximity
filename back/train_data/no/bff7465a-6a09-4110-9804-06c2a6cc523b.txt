ankara ( reuters ) - turkey s bddk banking regulator urged the public on saturday to ignore rumors about financial institutions , saying turkey s banks were functioning well .
the bddk s statement appeared to be a response to a report by the haberturk newspaper saying that six turkish banks could face large fines from u.s. regulators over alleged violation of sanctions with iran .
it has been brought to the public s attention that stories , that are rumors in nature , about our banks are not based on documents or facts , and should not heeded , it said .
