manchester , england ( reuters ) - british finance minister philip hammond said on monday catalonia s independence referendum that turned violent was a matter for the spanish government , but urged restraint .
these are internal events for spain and for the spanish government and the spanish people .
obviously we deplore violence wherever it occurs , we want to see any constitutional questions wherever they arise settled by constitutional means , hammond told britain s lbc radio .
it s very important to maintain the rule of law everywhere , we would always urge restraint in the way that is done but it s for the spanish people and spanish government to resolve these issues .
