( reuters ) - the u.s. environmental protection agency has issued waivers on certain federal requirements for the sale , production and blending of gasoline to avoid supply shortfalls in the aftermath of tropical storm harvey and as hurricane irma approaches florida , the agency said thursday .
the epa said winter-grade gasoline will be allowed to be sold through september 15 in most states in the country other than those on the west coast and in the rocky mountains .
it also temporarily waived federal requirements covering the production and blending of winter gasoline through september 26 , it said .
