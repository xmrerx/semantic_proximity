vienna ( reuters ) - the austrian far-right freedom party ( fpo ) has agreed to rule out a referendum on austria leaving the european union as part of a coalition deal with conservatives led by sebastian kurz , fpo leader heinz-christian strache said on saturday .
at a joint news conference with kurz on details of their coalition deal , strache said the two had agreed to expand direct democracy progressively .
kurz also said austria s stance on russian sanctions would not change with the pro-moscow fpo joining the government .
