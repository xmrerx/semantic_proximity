NEW YORK (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Tuesday he thought the dispute between Qatar and some of its Arab neighbors would be resolved quickly.
We are right now in a situation where we re trying to solve a problem in the Middle East.
And I think we ll get it solved, I have a very strong feeling that it will be solved pretty quickly, Trump said in New York as he met Qatar s Emir Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al-Thani.
