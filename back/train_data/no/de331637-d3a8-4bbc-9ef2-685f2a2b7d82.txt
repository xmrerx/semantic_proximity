seoul ( reuters ) - south korea said on thursday practical and forceful measures than can inflict pain on north korea should be included in u.n. sanctions , a new batch of which have yet to be announced .
foreign ministry spokesman cho june-hyuck told reporters the measures should be taken in addition to surely severing funds that can be used for the north s programs to develop weapons of mass destruction .
the united states wants the u.n. security council to impose an oil embargo on north korea , ban its exports of textiles and the hiring of north korean laborers abroad , and subject leader kim jong un to an asset freeze and travel ban , according to a draft resolution seen by reuters on wednesday .
