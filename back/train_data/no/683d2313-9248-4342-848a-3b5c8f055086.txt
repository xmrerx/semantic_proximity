ankara ( reuters ) - president tayyip erdogan said on sunday turkey was implementing a deal agreed with russia and iran to reduce violence in syria s northern province of idlib , in cooperation with free syrian army rebel fighters .
now we are applying the astana decisions in idlib , erdogan said , referring to an agreement announced last month in the kazakhstan capital .
he said if turkey had not acted bombs would fall on our cities .
our efforts in idlib are going on , in cooperation with the free syria army , without problems at the moment , he told members of his ruling ak party at a meeting in the western turkish province of afyon .
