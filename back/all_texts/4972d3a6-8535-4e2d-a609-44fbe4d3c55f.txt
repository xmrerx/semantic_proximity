At 12:15 am at Seven Sister Road in Finsbury Park, London, a van plowed into a crowd of worshippers leaving a mosque.
Muslims were coming out from midnight prayers at the mosque when the van hit and injured about 10 people.
The suspect was pinned down by a crowd and kept on the ground until the police could arrive.
